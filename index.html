Thanks A Billion

Thanks A Billion was a Target Campaign at thanksabillion.target.com as well as facebook. Users left thank-you notes for a special teachers. When a user would tag a qualifying school in their message, Target gave that school $25 – up to $25,000 per school – until Target donated $5 million. (Even Secretary of Education Arne Duncan gave teachers a special shout-out.) The donations helped schools afford resources to help kids succeed.

Client JS

If you want to fingerprint browsers, you are probably also interested in other client-based information, such as screen resolution, operating system, browser type, device type, and much more.

Benefits Compliance Link

All tax-advantaged benefit plans must demonstrate nondiscrimination in terms of eligibility and benefits. Benefits Compliance Link can evaluate your plan’s compliance and provide assistance in meeting requirements.

Our online system allows you to upload your plan census and test for compliance with IRC Section 105(h) and IRC Section 125.

Most benefit plans need to be in writing, with clear, concise, and easily understood summaries for all eligible employees. Benefits Compliance Link can create your Premium Only Plan, Wrap Plan, 105/HRA plan, and Summaries of Benefits and Coverage (SBC) documents instantly. We can also generate 125 Cafeteria plan documents within two (2) business days.
